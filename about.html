About

Earlobe is a website and weekly newsletter dedicated to listing experimental music and sound art events happening in Toronto, Canada.

Founded in 2025, Earlobe strives to be a communal hub where organizers can promote their events, and fans of experimental music can stay in the loop without relying on social media.

Sign up for our weekly newsletter, or submit your event for the impending launch!
